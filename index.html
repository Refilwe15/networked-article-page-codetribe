Learn Networking and Servers

A complete guide to building, securing, and managing networks

What are networks and servers?

A network is a group of connected computers and devices that share data, and a server provides services or data to other computers known as clients.

Wi-Fi USB Dongles

A small device plugged into a computer's USB port to give wireless internet access.

Secure Online Collaboration

The use of encrypted tools and platforms for safe file sharing—like End-to-End Encryption.

Types and Functions of Networking and Wireless Connections

PAN (Personal Area Network)

Connects devices in a small area like your home. It's fast and secure.

LAN (Local Area Network)

Used in places like schools or offices to quickly connect devices within a limited area.

WAN (Wide Area Network)

Covers large geographical areas, even across cities or countries—like the internet.
